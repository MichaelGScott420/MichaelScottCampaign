Platform

Seven issue stances that shape the campaign’s social and economic direction.

🌍 Foreign Policy: Limited Intervention

Keep America strong and smart: defend allies, deter threats, and prioritize diplomacy. Use military force only when it’s absolutely necessary, clearly defined, and accountable.

“Talk first. Act decisively if we must.”

🎓 Education: School Choice

Empower families with more high-quality options — public, charter, magnet, and vocational paths — while protecting transparency, accessibility, and basic standards so every kid can thrive.

Choice + standards + fairness

🏳️‍🌈 Gay Marriage: Equal Rights

Equal rights are non-negotiable. Protect marriage equality and ensure LGBTQ+ Americans can live, work, and build families without discrimination.

Freedom means everyone

🩺 Healthcare: Universal Healthcare

Healthcare is a right, not a perk. Build a system that covers everyone, controls costs, improves outcomes, and reduces the paperwork nightmare (Michael loves paper, but not that kind).

Coverage for all

🌲 Conservation: Moderate Conservationist

Protect air, water, and public lands with pragmatic policies that also respect jobs and local economies. Invest in resilience, efficiency, and clean innovation without “all-or-nothing” extremes.

Practical stewardship

⚖️ Abortion: Safe, Legal, Rare

Support access to safe and legal abortion while reducing unintended pregnancies through education, healthcare access, and support for families — focusing on compassion and real-world outcomes.

Access + prevention + support

🛡️ Gun Policy: Background Checks

Respect responsible ownership and protect communities with universal background checks, improved enforcement, and closing obvious loopholes — safety without demonizing lawful owners.

Rights + responsibility

💰 Fiscal Solvency: Debt Hawk

Be honest about budgets: pay for what we pass, cut waste, and invest where returns are real. Strong public services and long-term fiscal responsibility can coexist — it’s not either/or.

Spend smart. Pay your bills.

Top Priorities

✅ Results, Not Drama

Focus on deliverables: coverage, safety, rights, and solvency — with less chaos and more follow-through.

🤝 Big Tent Democrat

Protect core freedoms while welcoming practical ideas (like school choice with guardrails).

🧾 Accountability

Clear goals, measurable progress, and responsible budgeting — because “bankrupt” is not a vibe.
